Our Commitment to Child Safety

Fourth is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users of the Fourth iQ app. We have a zero-tolerance policy for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior.

The safety and protection of children is of paramount importance to us. We are dedicated to preventing, identifying, and removing any content or behavior that may endanger or exploit children.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following activities are strictly prohibited on the Fourth platform:

1. Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, solicits, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation is absolutely forbidden
  • Content that sexualizes minors or depicts minors in a sexual manner
  • Any imagery, videos, or text that depicts or describes sexual abuse of children
  • Content that encourages or normalizes sexual attraction to children

2. Child Endangerment

  • Content that endangers the physical, emotional, or psychological well-being of children
  • Content depicting or promoting physical abuse of minors
  • Content that facilitates child exploitation or trafficking

3. Inappropriate Content Involving Minors

  • Any sexually explicit or suggestive content involving individuals under the age of 18
  • Predatory behavior or grooming activities targeting minors
  • Requests for sexual or intimate content from minors
Important: We define a minor as any individual under the age of 18 years.

Detection and Prevention

We employ multiple methods to protect children on our platform:

  • Automated content detection systems and filters
  • Manual content review processes
  • User reporting mechanisms
  • Proactive monitoring of flagged accounts and content
  • Regular security audits and policy reviews

Enforcement Actions

Violations of this Child Safety Policy will result in immediate action:

  1. Immediate Removal: Any content violating this policy will be immediately removed from our platform
  2. Account Suspension: Users who violate this policy will have their accounts immediately suspended or permanently terminated
  3. Law Enforcement Reporting: We report all instances of CSAE content to:
    • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
    • Appropriate local, national, and international law enforcement authorities
  4. Legal Action: We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations and may pursue legal action against violators

Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any content or behavior that violates this Child Safety Policy, we urge you to report it immediately.

How to Report:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the built-in report function within the Fourth app
  • Email: support@fourth.com
  • Emergency: If a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement immediately

All reports are treated with the utmost seriousness and confidentiality. We review all reports promptly and take appropriate action.

Cooperation with Authorities

We maintain close relationships with law enforcement agencies and child safety organizations worldwide. We are committed to:

  • Complying with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child safety
  • Reporting CSAE content to NCMEC and other appropriate authorities
  • Providing information to law enforcement as permitted by law
  • Participating in industry initiatives to combat online child exploitation

Policy Updates

We may update this Child Safety Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
